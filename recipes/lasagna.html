Lasagna

Description

We present to you some MAJOR sagna action. A staple in the Italian-American kitchen. Favorite of America's Favorite Feline, Cat J. Garfield. Layered Perfection.

Ingredients

  • MEAT
  • Garlic & onion
  • Tomato sauce: UP TO YOU
  • Sugar
  • Spices: salt, peppa, basil, parsely, italian seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese: ricotta, parmesan, mozzarella
  • love

How to Make

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
